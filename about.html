About Mizan

Modernizing Islamic finance through innovative technology

Mizan Software was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between traditional Islamic finance principles and modern technological innovation. We recognized that while Islamic finance is one of the fastest-growing sectors globally, its technological infrastructure has not kept pace with the digital transformation happening in conventional banking.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide financial institutions with the tools they need to operate efficiently, securely, and in full compliance with Sharia principles. We believe that technology should serve ethics, and that automation can strengthen rather than compromise the ethical foundations of Islamic finance.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Islamic financial institutions can compete on equal footing with conventional banks in terms of speed, efficiency, and customer experience, while maintaining their unique ethical standards. Through our platform, we aim to make Sharia-compliant financial services more accessible to millions of people worldwide.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest standards of ethical conduct in all our operations
  • Innovation: We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in fintech
  • Compliance: We ensure 100% adherence to AAOIFI standards and Sharia principles
  • Transparency: We believe in open communication and clear processes
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our platform and service

Our Team

Mizan is built by a diverse team of financial experts, software engineers, and Islamic finance scholars. Our team combines deep domain expertise with cutting-edge technical skills, ensuring that our solutions are both technically robust and financially sound.

Incubated at Cité de l'Innovation, we benefit from a supportive ecosystem that fosters innovation and entrepreneurship. This environment has enabled us to develop and refine our platform while maintaining close connections with the Islamic finance community.
