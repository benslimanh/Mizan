Pricing

Flexible pricing plans designed for Islamic financial institutions of all sizes

At Mizan, we understand that every financial institution has unique needs and requirements. Our pricing structure is designed to be transparent, flexible, and scalable, ensuring that you only pay for what you need while having the option to grow as your operations expand.

Enterprise Plans

Our enterprise pricing is customized based on your institution's size, transaction volume, and specific requirements. We work closely with your team to create a pricing plan that aligns with your business objectives and budget constraints.

Enterprise plans include dedicated support, custom integrations, priority feature requests, and comprehensive training for your team. All plans come with our standard SLA guarantees and 24/7 technical support.

Pricing Factors

  • Number of transactions processed monthly
  • Number of users and administrators
  • Required integrations with existing systems
  • Level of customization needed
  • Support and maintenance requirements
  • Training and onboarding services

Getting Started

To receive a customized quote for your institution, please contact our sales team. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your specific needs and provide a detailed pricing proposal tailored to your requirements.

We offer flexible payment terms and can accommodate various billing cycles. Our team is committed to finding a pricing solution that works for your organization.
