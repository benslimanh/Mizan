Careers

Join us in building the future of Islamic finance technology

At Mizan, we're building something meaningful. We're not just creating software—we're modernizing an entire industry while preserving its ethical foundations. If you're passionate about fintech, Islamic finance, or using technology to make a positive impact, we'd love to hear from you.

Why Work at Mizan?

Working at Mizan means being part of a mission-driven team that's solving real-world problems. You'll work alongside talented engineers, financial experts, and Islamic finance scholars who are all committed to excellence and innovation.

  • Competitive compensation and benefits packages
  • Flexible working arrangements and remote options
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development
  • Work on cutting-edge technology in a growing industry
  • Collaborative and inclusive work environment
  • Make a meaningful impact on the Islamic finance sector

Open Positions

We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team. Current openings include positions in software engineering, product management, Islamic finance consulting, customer success, and more.

Even if you don't see a position that matches your skills, we encourage you to reach out. We're growing rapidly and are always interested in connecting with exceptional talent.

How to Apply

To apply for a position or express interest in future opportunities, please send your resume and cover letter to our careers email. In your cover letter, please tell us why you're interested in Mizan and how your background aligns with our mission.

We review applications on a rolling basis and will contact qualified candidates for interviews. We're committed to building a diverse team and welcome applications from all backgrounds.
